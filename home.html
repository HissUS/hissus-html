About HISS US

​HISS US started in 2017, HISS US wanted to offer a product that provides a safe, effective, and stylish way to enjoy the natural flow of air and light without worrying about annoying bugs, dust, or peeping eyes. Drawing from years of experience living overseas, the team was inspired by the unique Pleated Retractable Insect Screen technology, which had won the Taiwan Excellence Award for its innovation. This remarkable product was introduced into homes and businesses across the United States, providing effective insect screen solutions for windows and doors.

With growing demand, HISS US expanded the business to the American market in 2017. HISS US has since become an innovative manufacturer that responds to market demands for security doors. We have created a series of products that blend art, luxury, natural breeze, and safety, resulting in security doors that are true works of art.

Our commitment extends beyond just manufacturing; we also provide hands-on training for our resellers, ensuring they are well-versed in both the product and installation. This comprehensive approach helps maintain the high standards that HISS US is known for, allowing us to continue delivering exceptional solutions to our customers.
